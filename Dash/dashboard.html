Overview

We are an integrated system that provides two interfaces for both the pharmacy and the patient.

Regarding the patient:
You can now have your prescriptions dispensed from the pharmacy and delivered to you wherever you are in less than an hour. This means you won't need to move from one pharmacy to another, escape traffic congestion, or endure long queues for what you need. We provide on demand delivery services from pharmacies to you.
Regarding the pharmacy:
pharmacy can add its Excel file and it will be saved in the dictionary. The pharmacy can also simply search for all its products and know their quantity. Everything you need is within a simple, user-friendly application
